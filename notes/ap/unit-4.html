LASERS

Intro

Lasers are coherent light sources which are produced through stimulated emission The acronym LASER stands for Light Amplification by Stimulated Emission of Radiation.

Characteristics of LASERs

  1. Directionality (Uni directional)
  2. High Intensity (More focused in a small area)
  3. Mono Chromaticity (One specific color)
  4. Coherent (High degree of coherence)

Requirements of a LASER

  1. Population Inversion
  2. Meta Stable State
  3. Active Medium (Medium in which the laser action takes place)
  4. Resonating Cavity
  5. Pumping (Large number of e-s from lower to higher)
