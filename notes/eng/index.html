Here are the key points what to eat:

  • Obesity is a serious problem and food intake and exercise are the two main factors that change our body weight.
  • A properly balanced diet should include whole grains, healthy fats and oils, fruits and vegetables, fish and eggs and dairy products.
  • Whole grains contain healthy carbohydrates which take a long time for the body to digest and can help prevent Type 2 diabetes and heart diseases.
  • Healthy fats found in olives, nuts, seeds, sunflower, pea nuts and fatty fish such as salmon help to control cholesterol and prevent heart diseases.
  • A diet rich in fruits and vegetables decreases the chances of having a heart attack or stroke, protects against some types of cancers, lowers blood pressure, and helps to avoid the painful intestinal ailment called diverticulitis.
  • Fish is an important source of protein and is rich in Omega – 3 fatty acids which help to prevent heart disease.
  • Dairy products are good for growing bones as they contain calcium and Vitamin D.
  • High levels of sodium found in potato chips, cheese, sauces, red meats and processed meats increase the risk of diabetes, heart diseases and colon cancer.
  • Foods high in sodium and refined grains, sugary drinks, sweets and salt cause an accelerated increase in blood sugar, which increases the risk of heart diseases and result in weight gain.
  • Adopting healthy food habits helps to look and feel better and energized.

Overall, the passage emphasizes the importance of eating a balanced and healthy diet to maintain good health and prevent diseases.
