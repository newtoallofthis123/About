Thoughts on Torrenting

Torrenting and peer-to-peer sharing are decentralized ways of sharing files between computers, making it difficult to control exclusive access or censor content. Torrenting is often considered illegal in many countries, as it enables piracy by allowing users to share files with others on the network. Is it really that bad?
